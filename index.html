About Tom & Colleen

A Legacy of Excellence
in Vocal Training

From the Stage to the Studio

Tom McKinney holds a Bachelor's Degree in Musical Education and Theatre and won the prestigious Metropolitan Opera competition in 1971. After a celebrated career as a performer, he dedicated himself to teaching the art and science of the voice.

Trained under the legendary Seth Riggs (vocal coach to Michael Jackson and Stevie Wonder) and his mentor Keith Davis of New York City, Tom developed his own groundbreaking approach to vocal technique.

Together with his wife Colleen, who brings over 30 years of professional performance experience, they have built one of the most respected vocal studios in the country, serving as the New Talent Development Vocal Technique Instructors for Music World Entertainment.

The Technique

A Method That Changes Everything

Sing With the Ease of Speech™

Tom McKinney's signature technique is built on a simple but transformative idea: singing should feel as natural and effortless as speaking. Since the method teaches how to sing rather than what to sing, it works across every genre of music.

Expand Your Range

Unlock notes you never thought possible by learning to use your full vocal instrument without strain.

Build Stamina

Develop the vocal endurance needed for long performances, recording sessions, and touring schedules.

Clarity & Tone

Refine your unique sound with precision control over tone quality, volume, and articulation.

Vocal Health

Eliminate vocal strain and disorders. Learn sustainable technique that protects your voice for a lifetime.
